# SIW Launch Readiness Plan — V1

## Status: READY FOR RESEARCH

The product is deployed and working. This plan defines what Auxesis needs to research and produce before we start distribution.

## What's Done
- Product live at smartinvoiceworkflow.com
- Auth0 login, Google OAuth, Stripe billing all working
- Daily cron processing invoices, creating Gmail drafts
- 6-stage escalation (7-42 days)
- Free tier (3/day) and Pro tier (100/day, $15/month)

## What Auxesis Needs to Produce

### 1. Distribution Research
- Where do freelancers who use Google Sheets for invoicing hang out?
- Which subreddits, Facebook groups, Slack communities, forums?
- What language do they use when talking about late payments?
- What posts/threads have gotten traction on this topic before?
- Who are the micro-influencers in the freelancer invoicing space?

### 2. Competitive Positioning
- What are the top 5 alternatives and their pricing?
- What's our one-line differentiator? ("Keep your spreadsheet" angle)
- Why would someone pick us over FreshBooks, Wave, or doing it manually?

### 3. Marketing Plan with Timeline
- Week 1: What channels to post on, what to say, what to measure
- Week 2: Adjust based on response, try next channels
- Week 3-4: Double down on what works or pivot
- Specific post drafts for Reddit (r/freelance, r/smallbusiness)
- Product Hunt launch prep (title, tagline, first comment)
- Indie Hackers "Show IH" post draft

### 4. Pricing Decision
- Current: $15/month. Research suggests $9/month is impulse-buy territory.
- Propose a launch price (possibly with limited-time offer)
- Free trial vs freemium vs limited-time discount — pick one and justify

### 5. Pre-Launch Checklist
- Switch Stripe to live keys
- Verify Google OAuth works for a real stranger (not just test accounts)
- Prepare support email (help@smartinvoiceworkflow.com)
- Write a "what to do if something breaks" runbook for launch day

## Success Metric
One stranger pays.
