How it works (The invisible assistant)

We designed this for non-technical business owners. No complex software, no new tools to learn. It just works behind the scenes.

1. Connect Your Sheet

Securely connect the Google Spreadsheet where you currently track your invoices. We only ask for the permissions we strictly need.

2. We Check Daily

Every single day, our system silently checks your sheet for overdue invoices. We automatically calculate the right time and tone for follow-ups.

3. You Review & Send

We don't send emails automatically (to protect your client relationships). We create Drafts in your Gmail. You review them and click "Send".

We send the weekly summary.
You relax.

To give you peace of mind that the system is working, we send you a quick summary email every Monday. It shows how many drafts we created and your total outstanding amount. No need to log into a dashboard.
