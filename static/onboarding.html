Connect Your Google Account

Smart Invoice needs access to your Google Sheets (to read invoices) and Gmail (to create email drafts).

We'll never send emails on your behalf. We only create drafts for you to review and send.

Select Your Invoice Sheet

Enter the Google Sheet ID where you track your invoices. You can find this in the sheet's URL.

Find this in your sheet URL: docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/SHEET_ID/edit

Make sure your sheet has these columns: Client Name, Invoice Number, Amount, Due Date, Status

Your Sender Information

This information will be used in your follow-up emails to personalize them.

Setup Complete!

Your invoice collection is now active. We'll check your sheet daily and create follow-up email drafts automatically.
