Privacy Policy

Smart Invoice Workflow collects only the information needed to operate invoice follow-up automation, account access, billing, and support.

Information we process

We may process account email, business name, selected Google Sheet metadata and invoice rows, Gmail draft creation metadata, billing status, support messages, and lead-capture email addresses submitted from the landing page.

Google data and Limited Use

Smart Invoice uses Google data only to provide user-facing invoice follow-up features you request. We do not sell Google user data or use it for advertising. Our use and transfer of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including Limited Use requirements.

Service providers

We use third-party providers such as Google, Auth0, Stripe, Resend, and Vercel to operate authentication, billing, email, hosting, and workflow features.

Your controls

You can revoke Google access from your Google Account security page. You can also contact us to request access, correction, or deletion of data where applicable.
